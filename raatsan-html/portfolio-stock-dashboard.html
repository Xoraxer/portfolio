Power BI Project

Stock Portfolio Dashboard

In this stock portfolio dashboard, you will be able to track your investing portfolio composed of Tesla, Apple, Amazon and NVIDIA shares with relevant KPIs such as dollar gain/loss using data from the SF API.

Research

One of the most common uses cases for data visualization in finance is the creation and management of investing portfolio dashboards. This is useful for institutional traders and retail investors alike.

KPIs

The KPIs that are being tracked in this dashboard are number of shares held, intraday change of the portfolio, dollar gain/loss ratio, and overall growth of the investments.

Design

The design consists of different cards for various KPIs, filters by stock, a pie chart representing the portfolio holdings percentage and a line chart detailing the stock price history.
